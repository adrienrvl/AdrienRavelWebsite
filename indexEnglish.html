Introduction

Passionate about electronics, I am currently a first-year Master's student at ECE (École Centrale d'Électronique) majoring in Embedded Systems. My enthusiasm for this field drives me to be consistently motivated, creative, and enjoy working in teams to find innovative solutions.

Currently, I am doing an internship at Somfy as an application developer. This internship allows me to apply my programming knowledge and contribute to tangible projects in the field of embedded systems. I am fortunate to work on exciting projects that help me develop my skills and gain valuable experience.

Outside of my studies and work, sports hold a significant place in my life. I have a passion for trail running and train diligently between 5 to 6 times a week. This demanding discipline allows me to push my limits, improve my performance, and find pleasure in competition.

News: Limitless Sporting Challenges

As part of my 2022 sports season, I embarked on three major goals: the Seoul Marathon, the 60 km run in Nice, and the 80 km SaintéLyon race. Unfortunately, the cancellation of the Seoul Marathon two weeks before my participation was an unexpected obstacle. Nevertheless, I tackled the other challenges with determination, successfully completing 2 out of 3 of my objectives.

The next challenges awaiting me are the 43 km run in the volcanoes of Auvergne, followed by the mythical GR20, a 180 km trail to be completed in 5 days. But my ambition doesn't stop there: I am also determined to participate in the Paris 10 km road race, the Paris Half Marathon, the Paris Marathon in April, and finally, the Marathon For All of the Olympics in August 2024.

This passion for sports reflects my thirst for exploring new horizons, pushing my limits, and experiencing new sensations. Each challenge represents an opportunity for me to surpass myself and live unique moments.
