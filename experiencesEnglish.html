2023: Internship: Software Engineer

Leveraging my programming skills in Python, C, C++, and HTML, I sought out the most suitable and user-friendly solutions to create an efficient software. This experience allowed me to deepen my knowledge in software development, enhance my organizational skills, and strengthen my proficiency in programming languages.
