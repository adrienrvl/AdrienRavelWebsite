Licences and certifications

2023: TOEIC - ETS Global

Score: 885/990 (Level B2). The TOEIC (Test of English for International Communication) - Listening and Reading Test is a globally recognized test that assesses proficiency in English listening and reading comprehension skills.


2021: Business Game Cesim

The Cesim Business Game is an interactive business simulation that has helped me develop my management skills and prepare for my future career. Through this hands-on experience, I was able to apply my theoretical knowledge, work in teams, develop communication skills, and enhance my decision-making abilities while learning from my mistakes. This immersive training provided me with a realistic perspective on the challenges and responsibilities I will face as a future manager or engineer.


2020: Projet Voltaire Certification

The Projet Voltaire certification has strengthened my spelling and written expression skills for effective professional communication. Through this training, I have improved my command of the French language, enhanced my writing skills, and gained official recognition for my spelling abilities. This certification is a significant asset for my academic and professional journey, setting me apart as a student/professional with strong language skills and excellent written proficiency.


2018: PSE1 (Level 1 First Aid Competency Certificate)

The PSE1 (Premier Secours en Équipe de niveau 1) certification has allowed me to develop first aid skills, including CPR, handling injuries, and managing medical emergencies. This certification acknowledges my abilities as a first responder and enables me to intervene effectively in case of a medical emergency, thereby contributing to the safety and well-being of others.
